## Your Task
Conduct thorough research and produce a structured research report. Focus on:
1. Current statistics, data, and trends (prefer 2024-2026 sources)
2. Specific facts an early-career tech professional in Australia would find useful
3. Surprising or counterintuitive findings that would make compelling LinkedIn content
4. Practical, actionable insights — not just theory

Prioritise these source types (in order):
- Peer-reviewed research and academic papers
- Government data (abs.gov.au, dewr.gov.au, immi.homeaffairs.gov.au)
- LinkedIn official reports and SEEK market insights
- Recognised Australian universities and industry bodies
