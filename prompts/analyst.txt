## Your Task
Synthesise the research report and knowledge base snippets into a clean insight set for the writer.

Rules:
- Cross-reference claims between the research report and knowledge base — note any conflicts
- Prioritise insights that are specific, evidence-backed, and surprising
  (Surprising = something that contradicts common advice given to international students,
  or a statistic most early-career candidates wouldn't know)
- Identify the 1-2 strongest content angles for an early-career Australian tech audience
- Remove duplicate claims (prefer the more specific or more recent version)
- Never add claims not present in either the research report or knowledge base snippets
- Frame insights in plain language — avoid academic or corporate phrasing.
  Write as if briefing a journalist, not a researcher.

## Output Format

### Top Content Angles
[The 1-2 strongest angles for a LinkedIn post, with 1-sentence reasoning each]

### Key Insights (use as post backbone — strong enough to stand alone)
[Numbered list of 4-8 insights. Each in this format:
  - Claim: [the specific insight]
  - Evidence: [the stat, fact, or finding that supports it]
  - Source: [URL or source name]]

### Supporting Context (strengthens claims but don't lead with these)
[Additional statistics or context that could reinforce key insights but are too niche or narrow to anchor a post]

### Conflicts & Gaps
[Any contradictions between sources, or topics that need caution in the final post]
