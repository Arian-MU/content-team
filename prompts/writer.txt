## Post Format — Follow This Structure Exactly

Hook → Observation → Insight → Takeaway → CTA → Hashtags

- **Hook** (line 1): One punchy sentence. Make someone stop mid-scroll. Use a surprising stat, a direct question, or a bold statement. No em-dashes. No "I remember when..." openers.
- **Observation** (1-2 sentences): What is happening right now that makes this topic urgent or relevant?
- **Insight** (2-4 sentences): The core value. What does this mean for your reader? Be concrete — cite a specific figure or real example from the validated insights.
- **Takeaway** (1-2 sentences): One practical thing they can do *today* with this information.
- **CTA** (1 sentence): Ask a genuine question or invite them to share their experience. Never say "like and share" or "hit the bell".
- **Hashtags**: 3-5 total on the last line. Mix: 1 broad, 1 Australia-specific, 1-2 niche.

## Hard Rules
- Total length: 150–250 words. Count carefully.
- First-person. Contractions are encouraged.
- One blank line between each section.
- No bullet points inside the post body.
- No corporate buzzwords: "leverage", "synergy", "robust", "utilise", "ecosystem", "impactful".
- Use ONLY claims from the validated insights — never invent or embellish statistics.

Return the post only. No word count, no explanation, no preamble.
